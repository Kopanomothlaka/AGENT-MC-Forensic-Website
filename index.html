About Us

All significant players and stakeholders interested in and involved in resolving commercial crime and other types of abuse in the commercial environment should be aware of the importance of coordinating and regulating South Africa's commercial forensics sector.

Our core services include forensic investigations, financial reporting and disclosure, governance assessment, and dispute resolution. We also focus on helping organizations optimize their operations through cost reduction, talent management, and technological integration, allowing businesses to achieve sustainable growth and efficiency.

Our Mission

AGENT-MC FORENSIC INC. aims to unify and self-regulate the South African commercial forensics community by coordinating initiatives to develop the industry and protecting the reputation of forensic practitioners domestically and internationally. The organization is committed to maintaining professional standards, delivering competent entry-level practitioners, and enhancing the skills of clients to add value in their workplaces. It also focuses on improving data quality for organizational performance, running programs for professional transformation, and facilitating community upliftment.

Additionally, AGENT-MC is dedicated to providing leadership on relevant business issues and building stakeholder confidence. As a self-regulatory body, it operates independently, without direct lobbying for specific industries or accreditations, and ensures a transparent, objective accreditation process. To be viable, it requires industry support, recognized expertise, and financial independence, all while enforcing serious consequences for non-compliance. Credibility is essential for the organization to fulfill its goals and gain market backing.

Our Vision and Values

To be globally aligned and a reputable self-regulator of the South African commercial forensic profession.The Institute, as the representative of the industry, will operate impartially and in the best interests of the sector and its professionals. Key values guiding its actions include collaboration, integrity, and objectivity, ensuring that it fosters a supportive environment for all experts within the field.
